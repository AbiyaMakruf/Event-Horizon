About Us

Our company produces coconut shell charcoal at least 125/mt for each product, we can increase production capacity according to market demand. We prioritize quality to be able to compete in the international market.

We are engaged in manufacturing where we process coconut derivative products, namely coconut shells into charcoal. the products we market are charcoal briquettes shisha and charcoal barberque.

CocoPeat

Cocopeat is considered an ideal growing medium. The obtained cocopeat powder is filtered, dried, dried. Cocopeat can be used for various planting needs. Coir is known for its natural rooting hormone and anti-fungal properties. We also ensure the safe packaging of cocopeat.

CocoFiber

Cocofiber is a crude fiber extracted from the fibrous outer shell of coconuts. Coir fiber is relatively water resistant and is one of the few natural fibers that is resistant to damage by salt water. Its fibers can also be used for various kinds of production.

Coconut Charcoal Briquette
BBQ Briquette

Coconut Charcoal Briquette is a compact block charcoal made from coconut shell charcoal. Our charcoal briquettes are made from 100% crushed coconut shell charcoal and molded using natural binders. As a fuel, it is hotter and lasts longer than ordinary charcoal. It is suitable for both indoor and outdoor household use. We have two types, namely bbq briquettes and shisha briquettes.
